## Information We Collect

### With Your Explicit Consent

If you consent to analytics tracking, we use Google Analytics to collect the following anonymized data:

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, navigation paths
- **Technical Data:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

### Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique visitors | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Maintains session state | 2 years |
| `analytics_consent` | This website | Stores your consent preference | Persistent |
| `analytics_consent_date` | This website | Stores when you gave consent | Persistent |

**Note:** Google Analytics cookies are only set if you explicitly consent to analytics tracking.

## Your Rights Under Quebec Law 25

Under Quebec's Law 25, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### Right to Information
You have the right to be informed about the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information. This policy fulfills that requirement.

### Right to Access
You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you.

### Right to Rectification
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.

### Right to Withdrawal of Consent
You may withdraw your consent to analytics tracking at any time by clicking "Manage Cookie Preferences" at the bottom of any page. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

### Right to Deletion
You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. Since we use anonymized analytics data, individual data cannot be identified or deleted. However, you can:
- Clear your browser cookies to remove the analytics identifier
- Withdraw consent to prevent future data collection

### Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used format. As analytics data is anonymized and aggregated, individual data is not available for portability.

## Managing Your Consent

### On This Website
- **To Give Consent:** Click "Accept Analytics" on the consent banner
- **To Withdraw Consent:** Click "Manage Cookie Preferences" at the bottom right of the page, then click "Decline"
- **To Change Your Choice:** Click "Manage Cookie Preferences" at any time

### Browser Settings
You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
- View and delete cookies
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Clear cookies when you close the browser

Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.
